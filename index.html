Format

  • Swiss System - 4 rounds
  • Everyone plays 4 matches total
  • No elimination
  • Est. time: 2.5-3 hours

Match Rules

  • Each match is 5 points
  • First to 5 wins the match

Pairing

  • Round 1: Random pairings
  • Rounds 2-4: Similar records face each other
  • Winners play winners
  • No rematches

Championship

  • Rank by most match wins
  • Top 2 players face off in a 7-point final match for 1st/2nd place
